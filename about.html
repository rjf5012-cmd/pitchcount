About PitchCountCalculator.com

PitchCountCalculator.com is a simple tool built for youth baseball coaches, parents, and league volunteers who want a quick way to manage pitch counts and rest days. Instead of flipping through rulebooks or charts, you can get an instant snapshot of how many pitches a player has thrown and when they can safely pitch again.

Why this site exists

Many youth coaches juggle lineups, substitutions, and pitch counts while trying to keep games fun and safe. This site makes pitch count tracking easier, especially on a phone during live games, so you can focus more on coaching and less on math.

What this site is (and isn’t)

  • Is: A quick reference and calculator for pitch counts and rest days.
  • Is not: Official league policy or medical advice.

Always follow your local league’s rules and listen to qualified medical professionals when it comes to player health and safety.
